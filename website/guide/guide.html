USER GUIDE

Hello, welcome to the user guide of my Vendor Updater program.
I will be going step by step with screenshots to guide you through.

Step 1

Run the vendor updater application and the program should be displayed on your screen. Click on "Open Master File".

Step 2

You will be displayed to choose an Excel File. Find the appropriate master file, select the file, and click "Open".

Step 3

Click on "Open Vendor File".

Step 4

You will be displayed to choose an Excel File. Find the appropriate vendor file, select the file, and click "Open".

Step 5

Click on "Save Location".

Step 6

You will be displayed to choose a directory. Select the folder you wish to save the updated vendor files, and click "Select Folder".

Step 7

After all files and save location have been selected, click on "Run". Two Excel files will be created for you ("master_vendor_output.xlsx" and "master_vendor_output_detail.xlsx").

WARNING: If no file(s) or save location is selected, an error will display. If invalid files are selected, an error will display. Make sure you follow the instructions.

DONE

Enjoy your new updated Master Vendor Files!
